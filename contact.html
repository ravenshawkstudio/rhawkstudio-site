Frequently Asked Questions

What is your design process?

My design process typically includes discovery, strategy, concept development, refinement, and implementation. I work collaboratively at each stage to ensure your vision is brought to life.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A brand identity project typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more comprehensive projects may take 2-3 months or more.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I work with clients globally. My collaborative process adapts well to remote communication through video calls, email, and project management tools.

What information do you need to start a project?

To begin, I'll need information about your business, goals, target audience, design preferences, and project timeline. I'll provide a detailed questionnaire to help gather this information.
